Ingredients
Steps
  1. Weigh out the fructose, maltodextrin, and pectin into a heat‑safe container.
  2. In a separate vessel, bring the water to a full boil.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the dry mix while whisking. Stir until the powders are completely dissolved and the gel is smooth.
  4. If using salt, stir it in while the gel is still hot so it dissolves evenly. Let the mixture sit for a few minutes to thicken.
  5. Let the gel cool to a safe temperature, then pour into your flask with a small funnel.

Flavor is optional but nice on long days: a splash of vanilla, a drop of peppermint, or a shot of very strong espresso all work well. Keep liquids minimal so you don't dilute the carbs too much.
