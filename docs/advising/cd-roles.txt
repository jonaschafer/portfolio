About the job
Thumbtack helps millions of people confidently care for their homes.

Thumbtack is the one app you need to take care of and improve your home — from personalized guidance to AI tools and a best-in-class hiring experience. Every day in every county of the U.S., people turn to Thumbtack to complete urgent repairs, seasonal maintenance and bigger improvements. Imagine what we’ll build together.

About The Brand & Comms Team

The Brand & Communications teams are the storytelling engine for Thumbtack building belief in who we are, what we do and the impact we make. Our team is the ‘emotional backbone’ of the company, comprised of strategists, designers, writers, producers, and both internal and external comms specialists. By combining strategic storytelling, bold creativity, and purposeful communication, we grow brand equity that strengthens demand, engagement, partnerships, and long-term trust–and we do it from the inside out.

About The Role

We’re looking for a Director, Creative Studio to lead the strategic direction, execution and the people who power Thumbtack’s Creative Studio, owning how the team’s creative ideas are translated into high-impact, multi-channel brand experiences at scale. This role sets the vision, priorities, and operating model for an integrated creative and production organization, translating company and brand strategy into clear team strategy and outcomes. As the senior leader accountable for creative quality, delivery performance, and operational excellence, this role ensures work is bold, on-brand, and delivered with rigor, consistency, and urgency. The Director, Creative Studio builds and evolves the people, systems, and culture required to deliver complex brand work end to end while adapting quickly to changing business needs. This is a leadership role designed for an integrated studio model, not a single-craft creative position.

What You'll Do

Set and own the strategic direction for the Creative Studio, translating company and brand strategy into clear priorities, roadmaps, and measurable outcomes
Serve as the single point of accountability for creative quality, delivery performance, and operational excellence across the studio
Lead and evolve an integrated creative and production organization, ensuring ideas move seamlessly from concept through execution at scale
Build, hire, and develop a high-performing team of senior creatives, producers, and people leaders, growing future leaders and shaping org design
Design and continuously improve systems, workflows, and operating rhythms that enable speed, clarity, and repeatable excellence
Influence senior leaders across Brand, Marketing, Product, and Commercial teams to shape ambitious, executable ideas and drive alignment without formal authority
Foster a culture of accountability, belonging, candid feedback, and resilience while role modeling Thumbtack’s values and leading through change

In order to be successful, you must bring

10+ years of experience in creative, production, and/or integrated studio or agency environments, with 4+ years of demonstrated people leadership experience
Experience leading and scaling multidisciplinary teams through clear structure and accountability
Proven track record owning end-to-end creative delivery–not just concept or design or copy direction
Deep understanding of production realities, timelines, budgets and executional complexity
Strong creative judgment across multiple disciplines and channels
Ability to balance creative ambition with operational discipline
Clear communicator and trusted partner who can align stakeholders and drive decisions

About the job
ABOUT RAIDE

Raide exists to elevate human-powered movement in the mountains. We design and build the highest-performing and most functional equipment and apparel for ski touring and running.

We are one of the fastest-growing brands in outdoor sports by defying industry norms and holding an uncompromising bar across product, storytelling, and how we operate internally. This is a rare opportunity to help build a generational brand from the ground up — where everything you create directly shapes how athletes experience the mountains.



ROLE OVERVIEW

Raide is looking for a Creative Director to define how the brand shows up across product, content, and physical experience — from packaging and retail displays to long-form storytelling and digital touchpoints.

This is not a traditional campaign-only creative role. You’ll operate at the intersection of product, brand, and performance culture, translating Raide’s design philosophy into a cohesive creative system that scales across channels.

You will define creative standards, guide art direction, and ensure everything we produce — from athlete content to wholesale assets — feels unmistakably Raide.

This role is both strategic and deeply hands-on. You will concept, direct, critique, and build. This is a unique opportunity to shape a brand that will change the way athletes approach skiing and running.

If you’ve been a CS supervisor or manager at a DTC or e-commerce brand and you’re looking for your next challenge at a company where your work actually matters, this is it.



RESPONSIBILITIES

Brand Vision & Creative Direction

Define and evolve Raide’s visual language and creative expression.
Establish brand systems and guardrails that ensure cohesion across all touchpoints.
Set and uphold the creative standard across product, digital, and physical environments.
Translate technical product innovation into clear, differentiated storytelling.

Physical Design & Product Expression

Design and implement Raide’s packaging systems.
Define seasonal color and print direction in collaboration with product.
Develop physical brand touchpoints across events, retail environments, and tradeshows.
Ensure the physical experience of the brand feels intentional and elevated.


Campaigns & Product Launches

Concept and direct seasonal launch campaigns (starting with SS26).
Lead creative development from idea through execution — including shoots and asset creation.
Craft culturally relevant ideas that position Raide as a leader in human-powered movement.


Content & Art Direction

Define the creative direction across web, social, email, and digital environments.
Build frameworks for athlete storytelling and long-form brand narratives.
Direct photo and video shoots in mountain environments.
Establish scalable creative systems that allow a lean team to execute consistently.


Creative Operations & Team Building

Work with freelance designers, photographers, filmmakers, and collaborators.
Build scalable creative workflows appropriate for a fast-growing startup.
Use technology and AI tools to operate lean while maintaining a high production standard.
Help define future internal creative hires as the brand scales.


First Projects

Define and formalize Raide’s creative world and visual systems.
Lead and execute the SS26 launch campaign.
Establish a cohesive visual product naming, product info and product collection system across web, social, and email.
Direct early content shoots.
Shape Raide’s presence at running and ski touring events.
Improve Raide’s packaging


WHO YOU ARE

7+ years in creative roles at high-growth consumer brands, agencies, or design studios.
Proven experience leading creative direction for product launches and campaigns.
Experience building or evolving a brand’s identity and systems.
Strong portfolio demonstrating unique world-building, visual taste, and storytelling ability.
Comfortable operating in lean, high-ownership startup environments.
Strong familiarity with skiing and running.


WHAT WE OFFER

Competitive salary ($140k–$200k depending on experience)
Performance bonuses
Equity
Free gear
Flexible working hours
Unlimited vacation
Health and dental insurance

Manage and hire a group of world-class designers

Advance your own skills at people management in a supportive and growth-oriented environment

5+ years of design management experience, ideally including managing managers

Clear hands-on expertise with Figma’s tools, and a strong ability to mentor and guide design teams. 
Experience influencing teams through strategic thinking and communication
Depth in the latest AI developments and modern tooling
Help lead teams with resilience and guide through ambiguity

Experience working on early stage or zero-to-one projects, and seeing them through into unambiguous market fit
The ability to thrive in a fast-paced, evolving environment, bringing a sense of humor and a proactive, solution-oriented approach. 
Comfort exploring and experimenting with new methodologies and continuously seeking to improve both personal and team performance.

We are looking for a Director of Design to lead the experience for our content ecosystem including community builders, our users, and our developers. You will nurture Reddit into a unique, thriving ecosystem of conversations that brings human experiences to life across any topic you can imagine, and cultivate belonging for users. You’ll work across three different areas, including our moderator-facing products, our user profile features for various user types, and our developer platform.

As a Director of Design, you will not only define a cohesive strategy across the Reddit consumer experience but also serve as a key pillar of the Reddit UX organization. You will be responsible for looking around the corners to forecast critical user needs and business needs, acting as a creative innovator and change driver to deliver significantly positive impact.

Operational Excellence: Own the quality of the user experience and the efficiency of the design organization by enhancing design systems and high-impact delivery workflows.
Executive Influence: Effectively communicate and influence at all levels across different functions, including senior executives, to champion user-centric solutions
Change Management: Exhibit a "Reddit-first" attitude, showing the ability to quickly change course or pivot functions to support rapidly changing business priorities.
